## Projects

### 1. Healthcare Analytics — Hospital Readmission Risk & Diabetes Care
Interactive dashboard analyzing hospital readmission and diabetes care indicators. Isolated the highest-risk cohorts — diabetes patients, inpatient stays of 8+ days, and poor A1C results — giving care teams a prioritized view for readmission reduction and cost control.

📊 [**Live Tableau Dashboard**](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/sravani.kadiyala/viz/HospitalReadmissionRiskDiabetesCare/Dashboard1) · [View Project](https://github.com/Sravani-droid/data-analytics-portfolio/tree/main/HealthCare-Analytics)

### 2. Supply Chain & Logistics Intelligence
Interrogated 180,000+ shipment records against a 90% on-time SLA assumption — validated the delivery-status logic and exposed a 45.2% actual on-time rate, tracing root causes by route and carrier and quantifying $3.8M in quarterly penalty exposure.

[View Project](https://github.com/Sravani-droid/data-analytics-portfolio/tree/main/SupplyChain-Analytics)

### 3. Marketing Analytics — Campaign Response Dashboard
Analyzed campaign performance and customer behavior: 14.9% overall campaign response rate and $605.80 average customer spend, with segment-level views that show which channels and customer groups actually convert.

[View Project](https://github.com/Sravani-droid/data-analytics-portfolio/tree/main/Marketing-Analytics)

### 4. Retail Analytics — Profitability Dashboard
Profitability analysis across product categories: Technology drives the strongest margins, furniture underperforms, and aggressive discounting measurably erodes profit — insights that guide pricing and category strategy.

[View Project](https://github.com/Sravani-droid/data-analytics-portfolio/tree/main/Retail-Analytics)

### 5. Finance Analytics — Banking Transaction Risk & Customer Behavior
Dashboard surfacing transaction risk patterns and customer behavior signals in banking data, supporting fraud-awareness and customer-segmentation decisions.

[View Project](https://github.com/Sravani-droid/data-analytics-portfolio/tree/main/Finance-Analytics)

### 6. HR Analytics — Workforce & Turnover Dashboard
Workforce analysis identifying turnover patterns by department and role, giving leadership an early-warning view of attrition risk.

[View Project](https://github.com/Sravani-droid/data-analytics-portfolio/tree/main/HR-Analytics)

### 7. SaaS Analytics — Retention & Funnel Dashboard
Subscription business analysis showing retention outweighs acquisition in revenue impact, with funnel drop-off points identified for product and growth teams.

[View Project](https://github.com/Sravani-droid/data-analytics-portfolio/tree/main/SaaS-Analytics)

## How each project is built

Every dashboard follows the same discipline: collect and clean multi-source raw data → validate against expected business logic → analyze for trends and root causes → present findings in an interactive dashboard a decision-maker can read in one minute. Each project folder contains its own README with the dataset, methodology, and key findings.
